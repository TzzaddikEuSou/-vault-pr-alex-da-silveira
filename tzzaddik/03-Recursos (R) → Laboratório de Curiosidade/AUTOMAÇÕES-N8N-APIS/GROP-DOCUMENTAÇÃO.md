# Welcome to LMNT

> Our text-to-speech and voice cloning API gives you the tools to build lifelike AI experiences

At LMNT, we're building next-level speech models that are fast, affordable, and
easy-to-use. With our API, you can **create voice clones** with just 5 seconds
of audio and **generate lifelike speech** in real-time. Our goal is to help you
add that human *element* to your applications with ease.

**Ready to start building with AI?** Give your tools full context on our API to get more accurate code generation.
